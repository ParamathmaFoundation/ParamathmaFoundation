WHO WE ARE

Paramathma is a foundation lies on promise made to work towards the betterment and development of the society. Our utmost service goal is to provide with the basic amenities for each and every person in the society who are deprived from it. Paramathma aims at lighting up hope in all during hard times of their life.

A best deed for human kind is always to stand by the people and ensure support for their livelihood. A helping hand always matters in serving the miracles for each and every person who struggle for their daily needs.

Paramathma delivers the necessities for all who struggle in their life for their daily survival.
